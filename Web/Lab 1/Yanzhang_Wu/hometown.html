Welcome to Dalian

City Information

  • Province: Liaoning
  • Location: Southern tip of Liaodong peninsula
  • Population: 7.5 million
  • China's most livable city (2016 by China Daily)

Dalian is a financial, shipping, and logistics center for Northeast China as well as East Asia. Due to its location, Dalian was colonized by Russia and Japan and was previously known as "Dalniy" (Russian: Дальний; Dal'nii), "Dairen" (Japanese: 大連), respectively. Nowadays, when you walk along the street on Dalian, you will find the buildings are in Russian styles, Japanese styles as well as modern styles.

In 2007, it was one of the three cities named "China's best tourist city", along with Hangzhou and Chengdu, recognized by the National Tourism Administration and the United Nations World Tourism Organization.

If you would like to know more about Dalian, plase refer to Wikipedia.
